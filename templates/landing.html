FAQ

Questions people ask before signing up

Didn't find your answer? Email us at 1techspace.mail@gmail.com .

Does Google know these reviews are AI-generated?
Reviews are written for customers, not by the AI on Google's side. The customer reads the suggestions, picks one, edits it if they want, and taps Submit themselves from their own phone. The review is their own post — we're just removing the friction of typing.
What happens to 1- and 2-star ratings?
By default, customers who give 1 or 2 stars never see the Google submit path. They see a private feedback form instead, and you get the message in your inbox. You control the threshold — you can set it to 1, 2, or 3 stars.
Will it work for my kind of shop?
Tapstar supports 68 Indian-market business types — restaurants, kirana stores, salons, clinics, hardware stores, hotels, tiffin services, tailors, petrol pumps, coaching classes and a lot more. Each type gets its own category chips and vocabulary so reviews talk about things that actually matter for that business.
Do customers need to install an app?
No. They scan your QR with their phone camera, and the review page opens in their browser. Takes about 10 seconds end-to-end.
What languages do you support?
English, Hinglish (Roman script — "bahut accha tha"), Hinglish in Devanagari ("बहुत accha था"), Hindi (हिंदी), Marathi (मराठी), and Minglish (Marathi-English mix). The AI auto-picks based on your customer's choice, or you can lock it to one.
How do I find my Google Place ID?
You don't have to. Open your business on Google Maps → tap Share → Copy link, then paste the whole URL into Tapstar. We resolve it to a Place ID automatically — even short maps.app.goo.gl links work.
How does billing work?
Monthly plans via Easebuzz — pay with UPI, cards, or net banking. No auto-debit mandate. You pay each month explicitly; skip a month and your plan pauses. Start on the free Starter plan and upgrade whenever.
Can I edit the review before submitting?
Yes. The customer sees 4 AI-generated options, picks one, and there's a text box below where they can tweak it freely. Or they can hit "Generate 4 more" if none of the options feel right.
